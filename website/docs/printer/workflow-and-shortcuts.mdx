## Shortcuts

| Shortcut  | Action | Remember by |
|-----------------|-----------------|-----------------|
| Ctrl+g | update job status to gesliced | 'g' stands for gesliced |
| Ctrl+a | update job status to aangezet | 'a' stands for aangezet |
| Ctrl+k | update job status to verwerkt | 'k' stands for klaar |
